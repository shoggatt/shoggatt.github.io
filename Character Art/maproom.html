Map Room

An animated portrait of a fox character in a room full of maps and treasure.

I drew and animated this piece in Clip Studio Paint. The motion of the fox's body is tweened, and the motion of their eyes and the flickering candles is frame-by-frame.

In this piece, I wanted to try making an immersive background with many elements besides the fox character. I also wanted to make the colors coehsive so that the background did not distract from the character.
